Paragraphs

Paragraphs - Proxima Nova, Arial, sans-serif

Proxima Nova is the primary paragraph typeface. This is a good fit for photography branding because of its modern, neutral, and highly readable nature. It's conveys a feeling of precision and technology. I will use it for product descriptions, specifications, and UI/UX elements to ensure clarity, functionality, and legibility.

Arial is the alternative typeface for paragraphs. This is a web-safe sans serif font that is clean, modern, and highly legible.


Headings - Didot, Georgia, serif

Didot is the primary typeface for Headings. Known for its elegance and luxury appeal, Didot is often associated with high-end, artistic branding. I will use this font as headings and titles.

Georgia is the alternative for headings. This is a web-safe serif font with a classic feel, and it’s highly readable and widely supported.
